## Key Observations
1. **All validated PLPs are Tier A.** Every confirmed product listing page on beterbed.nl scored ≥ 56 — all are nav-linked with depth 1 or 2 and carry real product inventory with active filter facets. The minimum floor is high because every page receives the nav-linked bonus (+30) and depth-1/2 bonus (+10–20).
2. **Sitemap contains only PDPs, no PLPs.** The sitemap.xml (1,000+ URLs) uses paths like `/dekbedovertrekken/dekbedovertrek-name-ID` — always with a product slug as the terminal segment. No PLP-level category entries are sitemapped; PLP discovery must rely entirely on the homepage nav crawl.
3. **Umbrella categories dominate by product count.** `/beddengoed` (193 products, all bedding textiles) and `/bijmeubelen` (182 products, all bedroom furniture) are super-category aggregators that outrank focused categories by raw volume. These are the broadest audit targets.
4. **Google visibility concentrates on core sleep categories.** `/boxsprings` earned the highest Google score (18 pts) across sampled queries, followed by `/beddengoed` and `/dekbedovertrekken` (16 pts each). These categories have the strongest measurable organic presence.
5. **Sub-category PLPs are genuine but narrow.** Pages like `/boxsprings/vlak` (39 products), `/matrassen/traagschuim` (31), and `/matrassen/pocketvering` (15) are fully functional PLPs with rich filter facets, appropriate for type-specific landing pages, but serve narrower audiences than their depth-1 parent categories.
