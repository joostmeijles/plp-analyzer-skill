## Key Observations

1. **Flat Dutch slug pattern is the exclusive PLP structure** — all category pages are depth-1 (e.g. `/verjaardagskaarten`) with subcategories at depth-2 (e.g. `/verjaardagskaarten/grappig`); no platform-specific prefix like `/collections/` is used.
2. **Verjaardagskaarten and geboortekaartjes are the twin pillars** — both tie at combined score 70, reflecting dominant search demand for birthday cards and birth announcements in the Netherlands.
3. **Strong seasonal category presence** — kerstkaarten, moederdag-kaarten, sinterklaaskaarten, and paaskaarten all rank in Tier A, showing the site's breadth across calendar events.
4. **JavaScript-rendered product grids** — card listings are client-side rendered; productCount could not be extracted from raw HTML.
5. **No Tier B gap** — scores jump directly from Tier A (nav-linked depth-1, score ≥51) to Tier C (non-nav depth-2, score ≤28), reflecting a clean two-tier site architecture.
